Who We Are

We are a dynamic and youth-led organization committed to fostering positive change in the lives of young individuals. Our organization is rooted in the belief that empowered youth can transform communities and contribute to the development of a thriving, inclusive society. At Youth Plus Elite Club, we are dedicated to meeting the multi-faceted needs of young people, through a youth-led model to foster their social and economic development.

Sexual and Reproductive Health(SRH) and Rights for young people are at the heart of our work as we stride to empower young people between 10 to 35 years to improve their sexual and reproductive health as well as their socio-economic situation. With our rich portfolio of activities tailored to empower young people to enjoy the fundamental right to make informed choices regarding their sexual reproductive wellbeing and also influence various stakeholders in this direction, we facilitate the achievement of broader development goals.

Our Vision

A world where every young person is equipped with the skills, knowledge & confidence to lead successful lives and shape their communities.

Our Mission

To empower young people through comprehensive programs in sexual and reproductive health & rights, leadership, entrepreneurship & life skills, enabling them to reach their full potential.

Our History

2018

Registration

We officially got registered as a community based organisation.

2014

New Partners

We joined the Kampala Empowerment Centre umbrella to work in consortia with youth-led CBOs to deliver on our mandate with support by DSW Uganda(Now Action 4 Health Uganda).

2013

Foundation

Youth Plus Uganda was founded in 2013 by two youths, Nakkungu Deborah and Mutebi Steven. Our first offices were at the LC1 chairman's offices in Mutundwe.
