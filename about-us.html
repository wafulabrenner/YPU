Who We Are

We are a dynamic and youth-led organization committed to fostering positive change in the lives of young individuals. Our organization is rooted in the belief that empowered youth can transform communities and contribute to the development of a thriving, inclusive society. At Youth Plus Elite Club, we are dedicated to meeting the multi-faceted needs of young people, through a youth-led model to foster their social and economic development.

Sexual and Reproductive Health(SRH) and Rights for young people are at the heart of our work as we stride to empower young people between 10 to 35 years to improve their sexual and reproductive health as well as their socio-economic situation. With our rich portfolio of activities tailored to empower young people to enjoy the fundamental right to make informed choices regarding their sexual reproductive wellbeing and also influence various stakeholders in this direction, we facilitate the achievement of broader development goals.

Our Vision

A world where every young person is equipped with the skills, knowledge & confidence to lead successful lives and shape their communities.

Our Mission

To empower young people through comprehensive programs in sexual and reproductive health & rights, leadership, entrepreneurship & life skills, enabling them to reach their full potential.

Our History

2025

Membership with Kampala Youth Empowerment Centre

In 2025, Youth Plus Elite Club established a new partnership with Anchors for Youth Afrika, aimed at jointly delivering impactful programs and services to empower youth. This partnership is expected to further enhance the organization's capacity to make a positive difference in the lives of young people.

2021

Partnership with DSW Uganda

In 2021, Youth Plus Elite Club officially registered with the Kampala Capital City Authority as a Community-Based Organization (CBO) operating in Kampala. This registration marked a significant milestone in the organization's growth and legitimacy.

2018

Membership with Kampala Youth Empowerment Centre

In 2018, Youth Plus Elite Club became a member of the Kampala Youth Empowerment Centre, an umbrella organization for youth-led Community-Based Organizations (CBOs) in Kampala. This affiliation strengthened their connection with Action 4 Health Uganda and Deutsche Stiftung Weltbevölkerung (DSW), further solidifying their commitment to youth empowerment.

2014

Partnership with DSW Uganda

In 2014, Youth Plus Elite Club secured its first partnership with DSW Uganda, a prominent organisation dedicated to global development. Together, they worked on projects like SHAPE(Social Economic Empowerment Project), aimed at improving the socio-economic lives of youth in Mutundwe and across Kampala.

2013

Foundation and early years

Youth Plus Elite Club was founded in 2013 by two passionate youth, Mutebi Steven and Nakkungu Deborah, with a vision to empower fellow youth in Mutundwe. The organisation's early years were marked by a strong commitment to uplifting the lives of young people in the community.
