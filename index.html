Who We Are

At Youth Plus Uganda, we are dedicated to championing sexual & reproductive health & rights, fostering leadership, Entrepreneurship & development of life skills among young people in an effort to drive their socio-economic dev't for the country in general.
Founded in 2013, our work is grounded in the belief that every young person has the right to access information & services they need to thrive & lead healthy fulfilling lives.

Our Mission

To empower young people through comprehensive programs in sexual and reproductive health & rights, leadership, entrepreneurship & life skills, enabling them to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

A world where every young person is equipped with the skills, knowledge & confidence to lead successful lives and shape their communities.

Building motivated resillient responsible youths.

Integrity, transparency, innovativeness and empowerment are some of the core values we feed the youths to shift from a constraining self-view to a self-perception that is more positive and enabling.
